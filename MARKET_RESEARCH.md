## 3. Revenue-Sharing / Profit-Sharing Token Models

### How Revenue-Share Works in Crypto

Revenue-sharing tokens allocate a portion of **gross revenue** to token holders, distinct from profit-sharing which distributes net profits. The distinction matters legally and economically.

### Existing Models & Structures

| Platform | Asset Type | Payout Method | Frequency | Typical Yield | Min Investment |
|----------|-----------|---------------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **RealT** | US rental properties | Stablecoin (USDC/xDAI) | Daily | 8-12% APY | $50 |
| **Lofty** | US rental properties | Stablecoin on Algorand | Daily | 5-8% APY | $50 |
| **Kinesis** | Gold transaction fees | Gold (KAU) | Monthly | Variable (low) | ~$85 (1g) |
| **Maple Finance** | Institutional lending | SYRUP/stablecoin | Continuous | 10.6% APY | Varies |
| **Goldfinch** | Emerging market credit | USDC | Continuous | 9-12% APY | Varies |

### Key Design Principles (from CFA Institute research)

1. **Revenue share is preferable to profit share** for trust -- revenue is harder to manipulate than profit
2. **Clear, auditable on-chain distributions** eliminate "black box" fears
3. **Fixed schedules** (daily, monthly, quarterly) create anticipation and habit
4. **Denominating payouts in a stable/tangible asset** (PAXG, USDC) reduces volatility fear

### Real Estate Tokenization -- Closest Analog to AYNI

**RealT Model (Gold Standard for Revenue-Share):**
- 970+ tokenized properties
- $150M+ in tokenized assets
- Daily rental income distribution via smart contracts
- 88% of users invest less than $5,000 (retail-dominant)
- Global access to US real estate
- Key success factors: **low minimum ($50), daily payouts, transparent property data**

**Lofty Model:**
- 150+ properties across 40 US markets
- $50M in tokenized value
- $50 minimum entry
- Daily rental yields + governance voting
- Built on Algorand for low fees

### AYNI Payout Structure Recommendations

Based on competitor analysis, AYNI should consider:
1. **Quarterly payouts in PAXG** (as planned) -- aligns with mining operation revenue cycles
2. **Show projected yield ranges clearly** (e.g., "estimated 8-15% APY from mining operations")
3. **On-chain distribution** via smart contracts for full transparency
4. **Payout dashboard** showing historical distributions, upcoming dates, and per-token earnings
5. **Revenue (not profit) basis** for distributions when possible -- simpler, more trustworthy

## 6. Regulatory Landscape for Security / Investment Tokens 2025-2026

### United States

| Development | Status | Impact on AYNI |
|-------------|--------|----------------|
| **GENIUS Act** (stablecoins) | Passed July 2025 | Establishes stablecoin regulatory framework, validates PAXG usage |
| **CLARITY Act** (market structure) | Passed House | Divides SEC/CFTC jurisdiction, defines security-to-commodity transition |
| **SEC enforcement shift** | Active | SEC dropped most non-fraud enforcement actions; shifted to guidance-based approach |
| **Reg D / Reg S exemptions** | Available | Revenue-sharing tokens likely classified as securities; Reg D (506c) for US accredited, Reg S for non-US |
| **Reg A+** | Available | Up to $75M raise from non-accredited investors with SEC qualification |
| **Reg CF** | Available | Up to $5M from retail investors via registered portals |

### European Union -- MiCA

| Development | Status | Impact on AYNI |

| **MiCA full enforcement** | July 1, 2026 deadline | Comprehensive crypto-asset regulation across all 27 EU member states |
| **CASP licensing** | 40+ licenses issued | Required for operating crypto services in EU |
| **Asset-Referenced Token rules** | Active | AYNI tokens may fall under ART classification if backed by gold reserves |
| **Prospectus requirements** | Active | Whitepaper requirements for crypto-asset offerings |

### Global Trends

- **Switzerland**: DLT Act provides favorable framework for tokenized securities
- **Singapore**: MAS provides clear guidance for digital payment tokens and security tokens
- **UAE/Dubai**: VARA framework -- increasingly crypto-friendly

### AYNI Regulatory Strategy Recommendations

## 10. Onboarding Best Practices for Investment/Fintech Apps

### Lessons from Top Platforms

#### Robinhood
- Sign-up in under 5 minutes
- Free stock reward on signup (instant gratification)
- Simple language throughout ("Invest in stocks" not "Execute equity trades")
- Progressive disclosure: basic view by default, advanced features available
- **Key insight**: "Every extra step in onboarding loses 20% of users"

#### Wealthfront
- Asks users to self-segment by experience level
- Collects goals, income, age before suggesting investments
- Offers free financial planning before requiring investment
- Risk tolerance assessment built into onboarding
- **Key insight**: "Personalization during onboarding increases conversion by 30%+"

#### eToro
- Social feed visible before account creation (see value first)
- Virtual portfolio for practice trading ($100K virtual money)
- Copy Trading explained in 3 steps during onboarding
- **Key insight**: "Letting users experience the product before committing reduces abandonment"

### Onboarding Funnel Optimization

| Step | Best Practice | Drop-off Risk |
|------|--------------|---------------|
| 1. Landing | Clear value prop: "Earn passive income from gold mining" | High -- must hook in 5 seconds |
| 2. Sign-up | Email/Google/Apple auth -- minimal fields | Medium -- keep under 3 fields |
| 3. KYC | Frame as security, not friction: "Verifying your identity protects your investment" | High -- 40%+ drop-off typical |
| 4. Education | Interactive "How AYNI works" in 3 slides | Low |
| 5. First deposit | Multiple fiat on-ramps (card, bank, Apple Pay) | Medium |
| 6. First purchase | Guided "Buy your first AYNI token" flow | Low if guided |
| 7. Dashboard | Immediately show projected earnings | Retention critical |

### AYNI-Specific Onboarding Recommendations

1. **Pre-KYC value delivery**: Let users explore the dashboard, see mining operation data, and use the yield calculator BEFORE requiring KYC
2. **Low minimum investment**: $50 or lower (following RealT/Lofty model -- 88% of RealT users invest <$5K)
3. **Instant gratification**: Show "Your first estimated quarterly payout: $X" immediately after purchase
4. **Mine connection**: During onboarding, show a 30-second video of the actual mining operation -- makes the investment tangible
5. **Progress indicator**: "Step 2 of 4" keeps users committed to completing the flow
6. **Fiat-first**: Allow credit card purchase of AYNI tokens directly -- no crypto wallet required for beginners
7. **Welcome bonus**: Small PAXG bonus for first investment (even $1 worth creates emotional connection)

### Key Statistic
Poor user onboarding is the #1 reason for customer churn, making up 23% of all app uninstalls.

### 1. UNIQUE POSITIONING
AYNI occupies an uncontested niche: no existing platform combines gold mining operations with tokenized revenue-share distributions. This is a genuine first-mover advantage.

### 2. TRUST IS THE PRODUCT
For first-time crypto investors, trust matters more than yield. Invest heavily in: third-party audits, proof of reserves, regulatory compliance, team transparency, and mining operation visibility.

### 3. GOLD IS THE PERFECT TRUST ANCHOR
Gold is universally understood, emotionally resonant, and perceived as safe. Lean into "Real gold. Real mines. Real income." messaging rather than crypto-native terminology.

### 4. LOW ENTRY, HIGH TRANSPARENCY
Follow the RealT model: $50 minimum, daily/quarterly transparent payouts, on-chain verification. 88% of RealT users invest under $5,000 -- the retail market is massive.

### 5. PAXG AS DISTRIBUTION TOKEN IS STRATEGIC
Using PAXG (NYDFS-regulated, Paxos-backed) for distributions borrows trust from an established, regulated entity. This is a significant advantage over creating a custom distribution token.

### 6. ONBOARDING MUST BE FRICTIONLESS
Let users explore before committing (pre-KYC value). Offer fiat on-ramps. Show projected earnings immediately. Every unnecessary step loses 20% of potential users.

### 7. REFERRAL PROGRAM IS HIGHEST-ROI ACQUISITION
Model after Kinesis: perpetual referral yields where referrers earn a small percentage of their referral's mining revenue. This creates aligned incentives and organic growth.

### 8. EDUCATION DRIVES ACQUISITION AND TRUST SIMULTANEOUSLY
A learn-to-earn program (cost: ~$1-2/user in PAXG) with gold mining education content serves dual purpose: acquires users AND builds the understanding needed for investment confidence.

### 9. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE IS NON-NEGOTIABLE
Assume security token classification. Start with Reg D 506(c) for US accredited investors + Reg S for international. Plan for MiCA compliance by July 2026 for EU access. The regulatory environment is becoming more favorable, not less.

### 10. SHOW THE MINE
The single most powerful trust-building and marketing asset is the mine itself. Drone footage, production reports, worker interviews, live dashboards of gold output -- this tangible connection to a real, operating gold mine is AYNI's ultimate competitive advantage over abstract DeFi yields and tokenized financial instruments.
